Why CryFox

Purpose-built controls, zero-leak policy

Your browser knows everything. CryFox ensures it stays that way.

Zero Telemetry

No remote experiments, analytics, or surprise pings. What runs on your machine is the code you installed.

Fingerprint Defense

Protects against canvas, audio, font, and hardware fingerprinting out of the box.

Isolated Workspaces

Split workspaces, personas, and tab groups into fully isolated sandboxes.

Oblivious DNS

Encrypted, anonymized DNS routing to keep lookups unlinked from you.

Security

Every workspace is isolated, extension access is scoped, and updates are signed and verified.

Per-workspace Storage

Separate storage keys and cookie boundaries for each workspace.

Policy Packs
