Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our services

3. Types of cookies we use

Necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot function without these cookies.

Analytics cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our site.

4. Managing your preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Manage options" on our cookie banner, or by contacting us.

5. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy here.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us at arcbase@tuta.io.
